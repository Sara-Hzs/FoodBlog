Recipe of the Month

AL PASTOR

Tacos Al Pastor were brought to Mexico by Lebanese immigrants and have become popular in the United States. This dish combines smoky pork with sweet pineapple, and is topped with cilantro on a small corn tortilla. Yum!

BARBACOA

Barbacoa tacos get their name from slow-cooking beef or lamb over an open fire or in a pit. They're typically served on special occasions, and once the meat is cooked, it makes a delicious filling for tacos and burritos.

Read More

BIRRIA

Birria is a mouth-watering Mexican dish that comes from Jalisco, and it's so tasty that you'll be hooked after your first bite! This delicious dish is traditionally a goat meat stew, but you can also use beef, lamb, or pork. Birria tacos are the perfect combination of sweet, sour, and spicy flavors that will keep you coming back for more. While they're usually reserved for special occasions, there's no reason you can't enjoy them any day of the week. So grab a taco, and dig in!

CARNE ASADA

Have you heard of Carne Asada? It's basically grilled meat that's oh-so-delicious! You can use different cuts like sirloin, tenderloin, rib, or skirt steak. To make it even more flavorful, you can marinate the meat in citrus juice and spices like cumin. Once cooked, you can chop it up into bite-sized pieces and enjoy it on its own or in a tortilla. Yum!

CAMPECHANOS

Campechano tacos are a meat lover's dream! They're made up of layers of different meats, usually spicy pork sausage, sliced beef, or chorizo. Some restaurants use a mix of these meats, which makes them even more delicious!

Read More

CHORIZO

Chorizo tacos are a tasty Mexican favorite. Unlike Spanish chorizo, Mexican chorizo is made from fresh ground pork that's flavored with spicy chili peppers. After removing the casing, the meat is cooked until it's crispy. You can even add some onions, peppers, or jalapenos for extra flavor and heat. It's perfect for those who love a little spice in their life!

Read More

COCHINITA PIBIL

Cochinita Pibil is a delicious Mexican dish that consists of slow-roasted, marinated pork. The pork is coated in an achiote paste and marinated in citric juices, giving it a delicious taste and tenderness. It's a popular dish from the Yucatan region in Mexico and can be served with salsa and red onions.

DE CANASTA/AL VAPOR

Canasta or Vapor Tacos are yummy steamed tacos served in a basket. Fill tortillas with tinga, beans, or potatoes, steam them, and keep them wrapped until it's time to eat. Great for entertaining guests!

Read More

LENGUA

Lengua means beef tongue in Spanish, but don't let that scare you off! This meat is mild and tastes like regular beef. Cook it slowly until it's tender and then chop it into small pieces. Layer the meat onto warm tortillas and add some tasty toppings like cilantro, salsa, and onion. Lengua tacos are a unique and delicious option that are sure to impress your friends and family.

Read More

TINGA

And finally, we have tinga tacos! These tacos are a great option if you're not a fan of lamb, goat, pork, or beef because they're made with deliciously seasoned chicken. The chicken is cooked in a sauce made from yummy ingredients like tomatoes, garlic, onions, oregano, and chipotle peppers, which gives it a smoky and flavorful taste. It's a classic dish in Mexican cuisine and is sure to satisfy your taste buds!
